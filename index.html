What I Do

I'm a cross-media designer, educator, speaker, and advocate working at the intersection of creativity, accessibility, and community. With 15 years in higher education and a passion for inclusive design, I help students, businesses, and communities build better experiences—across print, digital, and interactive platforms.

Whether I'm teaching in the classroom, leading a global design workshop, or building brand systems through my consultancy, every project I take on centers people, equity, and impact.
